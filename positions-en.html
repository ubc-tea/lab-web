Postdoctoral & PhD Openings

Dr. Xiaoxiao Li’s Research Group — University of British Columbia (Vancouver)

Build trustworthy, high-impact AI with us.

Dr. Xiaoxiao Li’s group at UBC are recruiting multiple Postdoctoral Fellows and PhD students across fundamental machine learning and AI for healthcare, chemical & environmental engineering. We have strong compute, excellent collaborators, and real-world problem partners.

Focus Areas

  • Application Thrusts:
    • AI safety or efficiency
    • Healthcare

PhD Students

  • Fundamental ML: optimization, generalization, robustness, efficiency, safety, alignment

  • AI for Chem/Env Eng: materials & membranes, process modeling, digital twin, scientific ML, world foundation model

Ideal Background

Postdoc Candidates

  • PhD in CS/EE/Chemistry (or related).
  • Strong publication record.
  • Hands-on experience with large-scale training, RL/LLM post-training, or generative modeling.

PhD Candidates
