The User Story

Meet Alex & Sarah

Like millions of others, they're searching for their first home. They're excited, but also stressed and disorganized.

Their weekend is a blur of open houses, scattered notes, and fading memories. "Which house had the big backyard? Was the kitchen in the Maple street house bigger?"

The Pain Points

Why is it so hard?

  • 1
    Misaligned Interests: Agents need to close deals. You need the right deal. This fundamental conflict creates a trust gap and pressure.
  • 2
    On-Site Amnesia: In the heat of a visit, it's easy to forget what to ask or what truly matters to you, leading to missed details.
  • 3
    Information Chaos: Juggling notes, photos, and memories from a dozen homes becomes impossible. “Which house had the great backyard again?”
  • 4
    Lack of Personal Analytics: No existing platform truly helps you analyze your own preferences against what you've seen to provide personalized, data-driven recommendations.

The Solution

HomeSeek AI

An AI-powered co-pilot that organizes your home search, empowers you during visits, and provides personalized, unbiased analysis to help you make the biggest decision of your life.
